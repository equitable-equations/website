What is Quarto?

Quarto
I introduce Quarto at the very start of my courses on R. Here’s why.
Author

Andrew Gard

Published

October 27, 2025

Quarto lets you write and save code while also adding text, images, and other content to contextualize what you’re doing. It makes it easy to create attractive public-facing versions of your data work. My advice is to always work inside of a Quarto document when building your R skills.
